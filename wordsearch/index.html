    Word Search: Find the Words

    Word Search is a game that consists of a square grid of letters. The aim of the game is to find and highlight all the words hidden in the puzzle. Words can be placed diagonally, horizontally, vertically, or backwards. You will find all hidden words in the list below the grid. Each word from the list will be cross-checked as soon as you highlight it in the grid. Can you find all the words in the shortest time?

    How to play the Word Search?

    1. Find the first word

      To start the game, you must find the first word in a grid of letters. You can see the list of words that are hidden in the puzzle under the grid of letters.

    2. Find the next words

      Keep looking for the next words in the grid. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, diagonally or backwards. If you get stuck, use the hint button in the upper right corner of the game menu.

    3. Solve the puzzle

      Find all the words in the grid to solve the puzzle. Once you do this, you will receive a winning message with your time. Remember that you can change the difficulty levels and also compete with your friends!

    About Word Search

    Word Search is a word game that consists of the letters of the words placed in a square shaped grid. The objective of this puzzle is to find all the words hidden inside the box. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Under each puzzle there is a list of hidden words and after you find a word, it will be crossed out (marked as solved).
    Word search is not only a great way to pass the time, but also a great exercise for keeping your brain fit. This game improves memory (both short and long term memory), logical and strategic thinking, expands vocabulary, and improves spelling skills.

    The History of Word Search

    The history of Word Search can be traced back to the late 1960s. It is believed that the first Word Search puzzle was created by Norman E. Gibbs, a teacher in Newport Beach, California. Gibbs created the game as a tool to help his students improve their vocabulary and spelling skills. He called the game "Word-Find" and it quickly became popular among his students and other teachers.
    Over the years, Word Search puzzles have evolved and expanded, appearing in newspapers, magazines, and books. With the rise of computers and the internet, Word Search has become a popular online game, with many websites and apps offering the ability to play the game or create custom puzzles.
    Today, Word Search is enjoyed by millions of people around the world, who play it for fun, education, or as a form of mental exercise. It has become a staple of puzzle games and continues to be a popular pastime for people of all ages.
